📘 Microstructure Playbook

Use the following rules to execute trades based on the Advanced Desk signals.

🔥 Playbook 1: Gamma Squeeze (Momentum)

  • Setup: Negative Net GEX (Dealer Short) + Delta Flow > +₹50L.
  • Logic: Dealers are short options and must buy stock as it rises to hedge, amplifying the upside.
  • Action: Wait for Spot Price > VAH. Buy slightly OTM Calls. Expect explosive, fast moves.

⚖️ Playbook 2: Mean Reversion (Range Trading)

  • Setup: Positive Net GEX (Dealer Long) + Spot Price is between VAH and VAL.
  • Logic: Dealers are long options and must sell rips/buy dips to hedge, compressing volatility.
  • Action: Fade the extremes. Sell Calls if price stalls at VAH. Sell Puts if price holds at VAL. Target the POC.

🧠 Playbook 3: Smart Money Flow

  • Setup: Skew is heavily negative (<-2%) but stock is dropping, OR Skew is heavily positive (>2%) but stock is rising.
  • Logic: Retail is moving the stock price, but institutional money is buying the opposite direction options (Smart Flow).
  • Action: Follow the Delta Flow. Never fight a strong +₹100L or -₹100L DNF regardless of chart patterns.
