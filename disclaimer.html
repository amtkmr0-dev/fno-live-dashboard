4. Derivatives & F&O Risk Warning

Trading in Futures & Options (F&O) and other derivatives is extremely risky. Derivatives carry non-linear payoff structures and can result in losses exceeding your initial margin deposit. Key risks include:

  • Leverage risk: Small adverse price movements can result in large percentage losses relative to margin
  • Time decay (Theta): Options lose value as expiry approaches, regardless of underlying price movement
  • Liquidity risk: Far OTM or illiquid contracts may be difficult to exit at fair prices
  • Volatility risk: Sudden spikes in implied volatility can dramatically affect option premiums
  • Gap risk: Overnight or weekend gaps can bypass stop-loss orders

Past performance of any strategy, signal, or analysis does not guarantee future returns. Only deploy risk capital — funds you can afford to lose entirely — in derivatives trading.

6. Paper Trading Disclaimer

Paper trades simulated on QUANTRA Terminal do not involve real money or real market orders. They are executed against simulated or delayed price feeds and do not reflect actual trading conditions. Specifically, paper trading does not account for:

  • Slippage — the difference between expected and actual execution price in live markets
  • Market impact — the effect of your own order on the market price
  • Liquidity constraints — inability to fill orders at desired quantity or price
  • Execution latency — delays between signal generation and order fill in live trading
  • Brokerage charges — STT, exchange fees, GST, and brokerage that reduce net returns

Paper trading performance is not indicative of live trading performance and should not be used as a basis for deploying real capital.
